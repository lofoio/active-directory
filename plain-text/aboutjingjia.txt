Hubei Jingjia Automotive Parts Co., Ltd., specializing in whole series of
Dongfeng commercial vehicle parts, is a private company. It currently has
three wholly-owned subsidiaries: Hubei Jingjia Automotive Parts Imp. & Exp.
Co., Ltd., Jinan Jingjia Automotive Parts Co., Ltd., and Shanxi Jingjia
Genuine Dongfeng Commercial Vehicle Parts Super Market.

Relying on Dongfeng Commercial Vehicle Co. (DFCV), serving Dongfeng commercial
vehicles, our company has become a guaranteed platform of Dongfeng commercial
vehicle parts in Shandong province and a franchised dealer of the main
factories of DFCV and dozens of other well-known factories. We are also a
franchised supplier of nearly one thousand Dongfeng commercial vehicle parts
dealers.

“Strength comes from the charm of services” is the philosophy of our company.
Over years, we are immersed in the services of pre-sales, sales and
after-sales. We have won consistent praise from our customers and the honnor
of “Reliable dealer of the national auto markets” and “Consumers satisfied
enterprise in Hubei Province”.

We adhere to the Sunshine Service principles of Dongfeng Motor Co. : "Care for
everyone, care for every vehicle", "Create opportunities for employees, create
profits for customers, create wealth for society". We are willing to work
together with our customers for Complementary advantages, mutual benefit and a
bright future.
